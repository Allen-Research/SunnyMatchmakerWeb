Our Values

Genuine Connection

We beleive that meaningful connections go beyond the superficial. This is why Sunny is built around matching people based on more than surface level qualities like food preferences and favorite colors. We aim to create deeper connections that will last. That's what differentiates us. We take connection personally.

Trust

Meaningful matches and relationship growth takes trust. You deserve to be listened to, so we've been working hard to build an app that takes into accound each user's background and individuality. Dating can be overwhelming, but Sunny is here to support its user's through the process. Trust Sunny. Trust the process. We are here to help you find your rock.

Communication

We believe that communication is key to nourish sprouting connections. Sunny encourages it's user's to match less and talk more giving each match a chance to click. We know that each user has something unique to offer and that sparks can take time. You never know who might surprise you.
