Meet The Band

October Allen

Lead Singer

October's powerful vocals and emotional delivery are the heart of The Daughters of Venus. She believes music is a way to connect with others and express the unspoken. For her, the band is a family and a platform to share her voice with the world.

Ava Mitchell

Lead Guitar

Ava's electrifying guitar riffs and solos bring energy and edge to the band's sound. She loves experimenting with new sounds and techniques, and for her, the band is a space to push creative boundaries and inspire others.

Jenny Mcmillan

Drums

Jenny's dynamic drumming drives the band's rhythm and energy. She believes that music is a universal language, and for her, the band is a way to connect with people on a deeper level and create unforgettable experiences.

Kiri

Bass

Kiri's groovy basslines anchor the band's sound and add depth to their music. She loves the collaborative nature of the band and sees it as a way to create something greater than the sum of its parts.

Ayeda

Keys

Ayeda's melodic keyboard playing adds texture and emotion to the band's music. She believes that music has the power to heal and inspire, and for her, the band is a way to share that magic with the world.

Kate Wenz

Manager

Kate is the backbone of The Daughters of Venus, ensuring everything runs smoothly behind the scenes. She believes in the band's mission and is passionate about helping them share their music with the world.
