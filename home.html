How Sunny Matchmaker Works

Meet Sunny

After setting up an account, Sunny Matchmaker gets to know a little about you and what you're looking for.

Profile

Sunny helps you set up a profile and even gives tips on ways to make you stand out and increase matching.

Options

Everyone gets access to a community of users to chat with and make connections.

Matchmaking

User's who use Sunny's Matchmaking capabilities get access to personalized matches created using Sunny's AI Matchmaking program which takes into account user's personality and preferences.

Connections Grow Here

When you chat with users you can track the growth of your connection through a sproutling icon next to your chat. This icon grows the more you two chat and there's a special rose icon for pairs that chat with eachother the most.

Tailored Matches

Sunny matchmaker gets better over time as we get to know you! We believe in fewer high quality matches and that everyone deserves to find that Sunny Match that will help them get through the rainy days.

Talk to Sunny

Throughout the process, Sunny is here to talk with you about matches, get feedback about how things are going, and help you navigate the app.

Nourish

Sunny Matchmaker gives tips on how to sprout connections and nourish them into a meaningful relationship.

Send a Friend

Chatting with someone who could be a match for a friend you know? Send a link to their profile.

New Technology

Sunny Matchmaker utilizes Large Language Models to create matches between users. By getting to know our user's through speech, Sunny is able to matchmake by personality compatibility. We get to know you and provide recomendations on par with that of a close friend or family member.
